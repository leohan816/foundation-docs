MD-02_POSITION:
ACCEPT

MD-02_REASONING:
The proposition places evidence at the cheapest gate capable of falsifying the claim. The rehearsal must prevent a scripted order/refund from being self-contradictory or economically false, but it need not prove sustained durability or operational safety. Reconciliation of every attempt means every attempt generated inside the bounded rehearsal, not a broader population claim. Crash/restart, missing-event detection, backup/restore, monitoring/containment, and repeated reliability evidence remain necessary before the higher claim, not before the first useful proof.

MD-02_EXACT_WORDING:
```text
GOLDEN_COMMERCE_REHEARSAL_MINIMUM_EVIDENCE:
- GOLDEN_ORDER_SANDBOX_EVIDENCE = PASS as a separately pinned result.
- GOLDEN_REVERSAL_SANDBOX_EVIDENCE = PASS as a separately pinned captured-payment refund result; a pre-capture void cannot substitute.
- Both results use the same pinned contract version, build/configuration, provider-backed sandbox, and bounded rehearsal data set.
- One correlation path links the material UI/API/database/provider/payment/order/inventory/refund/operator records used by each claim.
- Every transaction attempt generated within the bounded rehearsal is reconciled; no unexplained money, stock, order, refund, identity, authorization, or operator discrepancy may coexist with PASS.
- The rehearsal record predeclares and passes only the immediate integrity and failure cases necessary to make its two scenario claims credible, including server-authoritative transaction binding, authorized customer/order/operator action, authentic and idempotent handling of the provider events exercised, and no duplicate economic or inventory effect.

DEFERRED_TO_NONPRODUCTION_BACKBONE_OR_OPERATIONAL_SAFETY_EVIDENCE:
- crash/restart durability;
- delayed or missing event detection;
- broader bounded-test-population reconciliation;
- monitoring, alerting, containment, and named response;
```

MD-04_POSITION:
ACCEPT

MD-04_REASONING:
The proposition is small enough to avoid an evidence bureaucracy and explicit enough to prevent promotion. Keeping `PASS` as the status and `CLAIM_CEILING` as a required separate field is clearer than encoding the ceiling inside a longer status name. `IN_PROGRESS_WITHIN_AUTHORITY` prevents ongoing work from implying broader authority. `EVIDENCE_INCOMPLETE` remains distinct from `EXTERNAL_PENDING`, so internal incompleteness cannot be disguised as an external dependency. The authorization field makes non-progression explicit.

MD-04_EXACT_WORDING:
```text
STATUS_SET:
- NOT_STARTED
- IN_PROGRESS_WITHIN_AUTHORITY
- EXTERNAL_PENDING
- EVIDENCE_INCOMPLETE
- PASS
- HOLD

MINIMUM_RESULT_RECORD:
RESULT_ID:
STATUS:
CLAIM_CEILING:
EXACT_EVIDENCE_PINS:
UNRESOLVED_IDS:
ACCOUNTABLE_OWNER:
REVIEW_DISPOSITION:
AUTHORIZATION_STATE:
NEXT_STAGE_AUTHORIZED: NO | SEPARATE_AUTHORIZATION_PIN

STATUS_RULES:
- NOT_STARTED means no result is claimed.
- IN_PROGRESS_WITHIN_AUTHORITY means work is underway only within the exact current authority and no PASS claim exists.
- EXTERNAL_PENDING means a named authoritative, vendor, counsel, or other external dependency prevents closure; completed work remains preserved and no automatic extension or inference is allowed.
- EVIDENCE_INCOMPLETE means required evidence is missing or insufficient; it is not PASS.
- PASS means the exact pinned evidence satisfies only the declared CLAIM_CEILING.
- HOLD means an unresolved blocker, failed predicate, unsafe condition, or required return decision prevents progression.
- PASS never grants implementation, exposure, release, risk acceptance, or next-stage authority. Any next stage requires its own separate authorization pin.
```

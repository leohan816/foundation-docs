INITIAL_POSITION:
The Round 1 position was PROCEED_WITH_CORRECTIONS at MEDIUM confidence. O1, bounded as Golden Order plus Golden Reversal with Foundation AI and Memory excluded, is the strongest current path to customer and commercial value. AGREED_FINDINGS:
- All eight reports converge on O1 rather than an AI/Memory-first direction, a synchronous Foundation checkout dependency, or a broad rewrite.
- Golden Order and Golden Reversal must remain separately evidenced parts of one bounded objective; sandbox, operational-safety, controlled-live, and Paid Beta evidence must not be promoted across claim ceilings.
- The arrow lists are scenario coverage, not a provider-independent prescription of transaction order.
- A captured-payment sandbox refund is the smallest representative economic reversal; a pre-capture void is useful evidence but is not equivalent.
- Founder, provider, counsel, contract, live, and beta decisions should be progressively frozen, with explicit reopen triggers and owner-independent work while external facts are pending.
- Selective reuse is a reversible presumption, not a fact. Planning evidence and, where still necessary, separately authorized implementation validation must be able to reverse it without forcing either a broad rewrite or sunk-cost reuse.
- State authority, economic reconciliation, recovery classes, security invariants, human-visible state, Foundation snapshot provenance, commercial-use rights, and a customer recovery path are necessary. Their strategic principles must be fixed early; most field-level and control-level detail belongs in later authorized work.
- Controlled live and invite-only Paid Beta are real commerce, not legal, security, accessibility, or operational exemptions. Full Public Launch self-service and generalized scale are not thereby pulled into O1.
- Evidence packages should be compact, decision-linked, and non-promotional. Documentation is not a substitute for executable evidence or customer learning.

DISAGREED_OR_NARROWED_FINDINGS:
- Do not make a full security annex, field-level data-lineage/lifecycle matrix, complete counsel packet, exhaustive Experience and Recovery Contract, or S0-S6 Execution Manifest a prerequisite to a planning-only Advisor mission. Strategy must require their bounded outcomes and route unresolved parameters; the Advisor and later qualified evidence owners must instantiate them before the affected contract or live gate.
- Do not make a disposable implementation spike a prerequisite to planning itself. Planning must first inspect admitted facts, identify the bounded reuse seam and migration unknowns, and predeclare falsification criteria. A spike requires separate authority and is used only where planning evidence cannot close a material reuse question.
- Do not turn the first sandbox loop into a reliability program. The first result is a sandbox rehearsal with a strict claim ceiling. Crash/restart, population reconciliation, restore, and repeated-run evidence increase proportionately at later gates.
- Do not import broad Public Launch self-service, localization, device, return, accessibility, generalized data-platform, multi-provider, or scale scope. Only the selected controlled path and actual Paid Beta promise must be usable, accessible, supportable, lawful, and recoverable.
- Do not require every Founder decision before planning. Some decisions shape research or contracts; others must be informed by planning, provider facts, counsel, or runtime evidence and close only before their affected freeze or exposure gate.

REASON_FOR_DISAGREEMENT:
The controls proposed by the other lenses identify real failure modes, but several reports express the eventual evidence artifact as if it must be fully authored before a planning-only mission. That risks recreating the documentation delay O1 is meant to end, deciding provider- or jurisdiction-specific detail without facts, and mistaking platform/control completeness for product progress. The smallest sufficient Strategy states non-negotiable invariants, claim ceilings, owners, evidence routes, and reopen/stop rules. Detailed schemas, controls, thresholds, counsel answers, test cases, and execution mechanics then close at the latest safe gate under the proper authority.

The default Council cycle remains three rounds:

1. blind independent review;
2. material cross-review;
3. focused rebuttal when required.

Rounds 4 and 5 are authorized only when, after Round 3, a material disagreement remains
that is decision-relevant and reasonably resolvable through new evidence, a materially
new argument, or a precise bounded clarification. They are not required and must not be
used to manufacture consensus, repeat stable positions, or delay closure.

The Strategist must record the exact reason before opening Round 4 or Round 5. No round
beyond five is authorized. This amendment does not authorize Council scope expansion,
Advisor dispatch, implementation, risk acceptance, product repository access, or any
change to the frozen Round 1 register or subject pin.

MD-02_POSITION:
ACCEPT_WITH_NARROWING
MD-02_REASONING:
The split between immediate integrity evidence and later durability or operational-safety evidence is sound, but “selected immediate integrity and failure cases” is too discretionary. It could permit a rehearsal to pass on two happy paths while a direct authorization, value-substitution, replay, binding, concurrency, or partial-failure case can still produce an unauthorized, duplicated, mispriced, misbound, or unreconciled money or stock effect in the same bounded loop. The narrowing fixes that unsafe consequence without moving crash/restart, delayed or missing event detection, broader-population reconciliation, backup/restore, monitoring/containment, or repeated reliability into the first rehearsal.
MD-02_EXACT_WORDING:
The first combined rehearsal requires separately passing Golden Order and captured-refund Golden Reversal under one pinned provider-backed sandbox contract, pinned end-to-end correlation, and reconciliation of every attempted transaction in the bounded rehearsal. Before `GOLDEN_COMMERCE_REHEARSAL: PASS`, it must also pass the immediately exercisable cases applicable to the selected loop that test: customer-to-order and privileged-action authorization; server-authoritative item, price, amount, currency, and stock values; provider-event authenticity, uniqueness, ordering, replay resistance, and binding to the correct transaction; concurrent last-stock behavior; payment-success with internal-order failure; and refund-success with internal-update failure. Crash/restart durability, delayed or missing event detection, broader bounded-population reconciliation, backup and restore, monitoring and containment, and repeated reliability evidence belong to the later `NONPRODUCTION_BACKBONE_EVIDENCE` or operational-safety predicate. No first-rehearsal result may claim those later properties.

MD-04_POSITION:
ACCEPT
MD-04_REASONING:
The proposed small status set is safe when every result carries an explicit claim ceiling and authorization state. `PASS` then classifies evidence only at that ceiling; it cannot be confused with exposure approval or automatic promotion. A separate `PASS_AT_DECLARED_CLAIM_CEILING` status would duplicate a mandatory record field without adding protection.
MD-04_EXACT_WORDING:
Use only `NOT_STARTED | IN_PROGRESS_WITHIN_AUTHORITY | EXTERNAL_PENDING | EVIDENCE_INCOMPLETE | PASS | HOLD`. Every result record must include the stage or milestone, status, declared claim ceiling, exact subject/environment/configuration and evidence pins, unresolved IDs, accountable owner, review disposition, and authorization state. `PASS` means only that the declared predicate passed at the recorded claim ceiling. It never authorizes exposure, changes an authorization state, establishes a higher claim, or progresses to another stage; every such action requires its own separately pinned decision and authorization.

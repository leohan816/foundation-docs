The proposition creates one unambiguous, non-promotional claim ladder without adding another delivery stage. `GOLDEN_COMMERCE_REHEARSAL` identifies the milestone, `SANDBOX_WALKING_SKELETON_EVIDENCE` caps the first combined result, and `NONPRODUCTION_BACKBONE_EVIDENCE` is reserved for a later bounded reliability/recovery predicate. MD-02_POSITION: ACCEPT_WITH_NARROWING
MD-02_REASONING:
The split between immediate rehearsal integrity and later reliability is correct, but “selected immediate integrity and failure cases” must be predeclared and minimally bounded. Otherwise a later planner could select only the happy path, omit replay/idempotency or partial-failure compensation, and still report rehearsal `PASS` despite a duplicate economic effect or ownerless recovery. The replacement below closes that unsafe ambiguity without pulling crash/restart, backup/restore, broad population testing, or a reliability program into the first proof.
MD-02_EXACT_WORDING:
`REHEARSAL_PREDICATE: The first combined GOLDEN_COMMERCE_REHEARSAL requires separate PASS results for one provider-backed sandbox Golden Order and one captured-payment sandbox Golden Reversal under the same declared contract/environment pins; end-to-end correlation; and reconciliation of every attempt generated by the rehearsal. Before execution, the later authorized plan must predeclare the bounded IMMEDIATE_REHEARSAL_INTEGRITY_SET. At minimum, for the selected path, that set covers customer/order and operator authorization for the actions exercised; server-authoritative amount, currency, SKU, stock decision, and payment/refund binding; provider-event authenticity, uniqueness, and idempotent retry; one deliberate duplicate or out-of-order event with no duplicate economic or inventory effect; and the order-side and refund-side partial-failure compensation paths used by the selected design. Crash/restart durability, delayed or missing-event detection, last-stock concurrency and broader bounded-population reconciliation, backup/restore, monitoring/containment, and repeated reliability evidence belong to NONPRODUCTION_BACKBONE_EVIDENCE or the later operational-safety gate. No numeric threshold is set by Council.`

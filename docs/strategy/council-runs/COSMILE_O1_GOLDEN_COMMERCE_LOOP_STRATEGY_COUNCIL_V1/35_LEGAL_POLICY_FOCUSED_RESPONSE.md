MD-01_POSITION:

MD-01_REASONING:
The proposition preserves the legally and commercially important distinction between a bounded sandbox demonstration and any claim of production eligibility, live readiness, legal or policy compliance, commercial validation, or progression authority. The later nonproduction-backbone claim remains conditioned on a separate reliability and recovery predicate.
MD-01_EXACT_WORDING:
The first combined provider-backed sandbox result SHALL be recorded as `GOLDEN_COMMERCE_REHEARSAL` with claim ceiling `SANDBOX_WALKING_SKELETON_EVIDENCE`. `NONPRODUCTION_BACKBONE_EVIDENCE` MAY be recorded only after the separately declared bounded reliability and recovery predicate passes. Both claims are limited to their pinned subject, configuration, environment, scope, and evidence. Neither claim establishes legal or policy compliance, production-provider eligibility, controlled-live readiness, Paid Beta readiness, commercial validation, risk acceptance, or authorization to enter another stage.

MD-02_POSITION:
ACCEPT_WITH_NARROWING
MD-02_REASONING:
The first rehearsal must prove the selected economic loop without being mislabeled as broader readiness. An unbounded reading of “selected immediate integrity and failure cases” would pull later reliability and operational-safety work into the first rehearsal, inflate scope, and blur the claim ceiling. The immediate cases therefore must be predeclared and limited to what is necessary to interpret the chosen rehearsal evidence truthfully.
MD-02_EXACT_WORDING:
`GOLDEN_COMMERCE_REHEARSAL: PASS` requires separately passing Golden Order and captured-payment Golden Reversal on the same pinned provider-backed sandbox contract and configuration; pinned end-to-end correlation; reconciliation of every attempt in the declared bounded rehearsal evidence set; and the predeclared immediate authorization, integrity, idempotency, and partial-failure cases necessary to interpret the selected loop. A pre-capture void MAY be lower-level evidence but SHALL NOT close Golden Reversal. Crash/restart and durability, delayed or missing event detection, broader bounded-population reconciliation, backup and restore, monitoring and containment, repeated reliability, and configured operational-safety evidence belong to the later `NONPRODUCTION_BACKBONE_EVIDENCE` or operational-safety gate as applicable. Rehearsal PASS does not establish legal or policy readiness or authorize live exposure.

MD-04_POSITION:
ACCEPT_WITH_NARROWING
MD-04_REASONING:
The six statuses and a compact evidence record preserve the Strategy-versus-later-detail boundary. The authorization and claim limits must be explicit because an unqualified `PASS` could otherwise be represented as legal or policy approval, release authority, production eligibility, or automatic progression.
MD-04_EXACT_WORDING:
Use statuses `NOT_STARTED | IN_PROGRESS_WITHIN_AUTHORITY | EXTERNAL_PENDING | EVIDENCE_INCOMPLETE | PASS | HOLD`. Every result record SHALL contain only the minimum common fields: result or stage identifier; status; declared claim ceiling; pinned subject, environment, configuration, and scope; exact evidence references; unresolved IDs; pass or hold basis; accountable owner; review disposition; authorization state; and `NEXT_STAGE_AUTHORIZED: NO | SEPARATE_PIN`. `PASS` means only that the pinned evidence satisfies the declared claim ceiling for the pinned subject, environment, configuration, and scope. It does not constitute legal or policy approval, qualified-counsel advice, risk acceptance, production-provider eligibility, release approval, or authorization to progress. Detailed S0–S6 manifests, provider fields, test parameters, and implementation evidence belong to the later authorized plan and evidence artifacts.

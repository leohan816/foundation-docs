MD-02_REASONING:
The split is correct, but the phrase `selected immediate integrity and failure cases` needs a selection rule. Without one, a team could cherry-pick easy cases and obtain a rehearsal pass while the same bounded attempt can still create an unauthorized, duplicated, misbound, or unreconciled money/stock effect. Conversely, requiring restart, missing-event, backup/restore, monitoring, and repeated-run proof now would turn the first useful proof into a reliability program.
MD-02_EXACT_WORDING:
`GOLDEN_COMMERCE_REHEARSAL: PASS` requires separate `PASS` results for Golden Order and captured-refund Golden Reversal under the same pinned contract version and provider-backed sandbox environment; end-to-end correlation for every generated attempt; reconciliation of every attempt in the bounded rehearsal; and deterministic immediate cases whose failure could make that selected attempt unauthorized, duplicate an economic or stock effect, bind an event to the wrong order, or leave an immediate payment/order/refund partial failure unreconciled. The rehearsal does not require process-crash/restart durability, delayed-or-missing-event detection, broader bounded-population or repeated-window reliability, backup/restore, operational monitoring/containment, or repeated-run thresholds. Those requirements gate `NONPRODUCTION_BACKBONE_EVIDENCE` or the later operational-safety result. A rehearsal pass remains only `SANDBOX_WALKING_SKELETON_EVIDENCE`.

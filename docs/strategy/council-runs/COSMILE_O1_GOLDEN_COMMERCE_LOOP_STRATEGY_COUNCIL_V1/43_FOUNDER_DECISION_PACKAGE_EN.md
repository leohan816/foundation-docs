## Recommended decision now

Approve the corrected O1 direction as the commercial planning basis:

- commerce first; Foundation AI and Memory V3 outside the Paid Beta critical path;
- selective reuse, not a broad rewrite;
- one bounded Golden Order and captured-payment Golden Reversal as the first integrated
  objective;
- evidence progresses from sandbox rehearsal to non-production backbone, controlled
  live, and Paid Beta without automatic promotion;
- exact providers and live exposure are selected only after current authoritative,
  vendor, counsel, and implementation evidence.

## Decisions Leo should make or narrow before research/planning

### D-01 — First market and currency

Options:

1. one domestic market and one currency;
2. one cross-border market and one currency;
3. multiple markets/currencies.

Recommendation: choose one market and one currency. Do not begin with multi-market
scope. The exact country must be Leo's decision because no market, entity, or provider
facts were admitted in this Council.

### D-02 — Initial SKU scope

Options:

1. one production-candidate SKU;
2. a curated 2–3 SKU set with the same supply/fulfillment/refund model;
3. broad catalog.

Recommendation: use one SKU unless two or three are necessary to test a materially
different commercial rule. Require authoritative supply, stock, rights, price, claim,
and Foundation-data evidence before controlled exposure.

### D-03 — Customer identity model

Options:

1. managed account-first identity;
2. guest checkout with later account linking;
3. self-built identity.

Recommendation: use a managed provider and the smallest account/recovery model that
preserves order ownership and support. Do not self-build authentication for Paid Beta.
Select the named provider only after country-specific official-source and vendor review.

```text
LEO_DIRECTION: MANAGED_ACCOUNT_FIRST | GUEST_FIRST | OTHER
NAMED_PROVIDER_DECISION: DEFER_UNTIL_AUTHORITATIVE_RESEARCH
```

### D-04 — PSP, payment, and refund model

Options:

1. direct PSP with provider-backed sandbox, verified webhooks, capture, refund, and
   settlement reconciliation;
2. merchant-of-record/platform model where it is commercially and legally suitable;
3. custom/self-built payment handling.

Recommendation: prefer a managed direct PSP if the selected entity/category/country can
be onboarded; keep merchant-of-record as a comparison option where it materially reduces
operating burden. Do not self-build payment processing. The first Golden Reversal must
use a captured-payment sandbox refund; void is supplementary evidence only.

```text
LEO_DIRECTION: DIRECT_PSP_FIRST | MERCHANT_OF_RECORD_COMPARISON | OTHER
NAMED_PROVIDER_DECISION: DEFER_UNTIL_VENDOR_AND_AUTHORITATIVE_RESEARCH
```

### D-05 — Inventory system of record

Options:

1. Cosmile database is authoritative for the bounded initial stock;
2. an existing supplier/warehouse/ERP is authoritative and Cosmile synchronizes;
3. operator-managed stock ledger with tightly bounded exposure.

Recommendation: use the real existing stock authority if one exists. If none exists and
the initial inventory is directly controlled and tiny, use Cosmile as the recorded
authority with named operator ownership and reconciliation. Do not maintain two silent
authorities.

```text
LEO_DECISION:
SYSTEM_OF_RECORD:
NAMED_OWNER:
```

### D-06 — Fulfillment, shipment, and tracking

Options:

1. operator-assisted fulfillment using a carrier portal and recorded tracking;
2. direct carrier API;
3. 3PL/fulfillment platform.

Recommendation: begin with the smallest reliable operator-assisted model when volume is
bounded, provided it is reachable, documented, auditable, reconciled, and customer state
is clear. Select API/3PL early only when actual volume, SLA, or supplier constraints
require it.

```text
LEO_DIRECTION: OPERATOR_ASSISTED | CARRIER_API | THIRD_PARTY_LOGISTICS
NAMED_OPERATOR:
```

### D-07 — Cancellation, refund, support, and reconciliation ownership

Options:

1. operator-assisted approval/execution with explicit customer request channels;
2. self-service workflow with operator exception handling;
3. hybrid by order state.

Recommendation: use operator-assisted or hybrid handling for the bounded first release.
Name separate authority for approval, refund execution, inventory treatment,
reconciliation, customer communication, and incident escalation; one person may hold
multiple roles, but no role may be implicit.

```text

CANCELLATION_OWNER:
```

## Decisions deliberately deferred

- exact identity, PSP, carrier/3PL, messaging, hosting, and monitoring providers until
  current country-specific authoritative research and vendor feasibility exist;
- exact schema, state machine, migration, transaction/compensation, and worktree design
  to a later authorized Advisor plan;
- numeric controlled-live and Paid Beta thresholds until the market, entity, SKU,
  provider, and operating model are known;
- live approval until Legal/accounting, vendor, security, runtime, human-use, and
  operational evidence passes;
- Foundation AI, SIASIU AI integration, retrieval, recommendation UI, and Memory V3.

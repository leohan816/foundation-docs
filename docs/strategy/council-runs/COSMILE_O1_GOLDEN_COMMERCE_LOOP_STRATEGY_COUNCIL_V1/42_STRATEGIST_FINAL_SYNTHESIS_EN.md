## Decision

O1 remains the right commercial direction. Cosmile should make ordinary commerce real
before Foundation AI or Memory V3 becomes a Paid Beta dependency. The smallest useful
implementation direction is a bounded Golden Order plus captured-payment Golden
Reversal, not a broad rewrite and not a feature-by-feature horizontal completion plan.

The original direction was strategically sound but overclaimed its first integrated
milestone as a proven backbone and grouped too many decisions before Advisor planning.
It also needed stronger reuse-falsification, human recovery, security, data-rights, and
evidence-promotion boundaries. Those corrections are accepted.

## Final claim ladder

1. `GOLDEN_COMMERCE_REHEARSAL` may earn only
   `SANDBOX_WALKING_SKELETON_EVIDENCE` after separate Golden Order and captured-refund
   Golden Reversal results pass under the same declared non-production context.
2. `NONPRODUCTION_BACKBONE_EVIDENCE` may be claimed only after separately declared
   bounded reliability, restart/recovery, missing-event, broader reconciliation,
   monitoring/containment, backup/restore, and repeatability predicates pass as
   applicable.
3. Controlled live and Paid Beta each require separate evidence and explicit Leo
   authorization. No `PASS` automatically progresses.

## Progressive decision sequence

### Gate A — before country/provider research

Leo selects or narrows the first market/currency, seller/entity path, initial SKU
shortlist, and the stage-specific learning objective. This is enough to begin current
official-source and vendor research; it is not implementation authority.

### Gate B — before planning-only Advisor work

Leo approves O1, exclusions, the Golden claim ladder, the reuse presumption, the
country/entity/SKU planning envelope, and whether the Advisor may return a bounded
component-replacement option when evidence falsifies reuse. Exact providers need not
all be selected yet.

### Gate C — before provider-specific contract freeze

Leo selects identity, PSP/payment/refund, inventory authority, fulfillment/tracking,
and operator/reconciliation models after authoritative and vendor facts are available.
Applicable counsel/accounting questions and participant/transaction classification
must be routed.

### Gate D — before controlled live

Leo approves named participants, exact limits, seller/entity and provider eligibility,
Legal/privacy/security/operational gates, recovery/support promise, and stop/rollback
authority after implementation evidence passes.

### Gate E — before Paid Beta

Leo approves cohort, transaction/order exposure, learning and exit criteria, support
coverage, incident ownership, and the next-decision rule. Public Launch remains separate.

## Reuse and bounded replacement

Existing Cosmile source should be reused where it satisfies the approved contracts.
Planning begins with read-only evidence and exact reuse hypotheses. A disposable
implementation spike requires separate authority and is justified only for residual
material uncertainty. If reuse fails a declared criterion, the Advisor may return a
bounded component-replacement option; no finding authorizes a whole-product rewrite.

## Evidence model

Use:

```text
NOT_STARTED | IN_PROGRESS_WITHIN_AUTHORITY | EXTERNAL_PENDING |
EVIDENCE_INCOMPLETE | PASS | HOLD
```

Each result records `RESULT_ID`, `STATUS`, `CLAIM_CEILING`,
`ENVIRONMENT_AND_EXPOSURE`, exact applicable subject/contract/build/configuration/data
and evidence pins, `UNRESOLVED_IDS`, accountable owner, review disposition, and
`AUTHORIZATION_STATE`. `PASS` means only that the declared predicate passed at the
declared ceiling. It never authorizes implementation, exposure, release, or risk.

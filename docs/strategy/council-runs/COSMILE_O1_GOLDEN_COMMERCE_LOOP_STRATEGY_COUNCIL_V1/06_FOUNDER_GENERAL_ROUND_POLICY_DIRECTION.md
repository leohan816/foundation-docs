## Policy

- Small or ordinary Council problems use a default maximum of three rounds.
- Difficult and materially important Council problems may use up to five rounds.
- More than five rounds require a new explicit Leo authorization requested in advance.

Before requesting a round beyond five, the Strategy Decision Architect must return:

```text
EXACT_UNRESOLVED_ISSUES:
WHY_THEY_ARE_MATERIAL:
NEW_EVIDENCE_OR_REASONING_EXPECTED:
WHY_EXISTING_ROUNDS_WERE_INSUFFICIENT:
PROPOSED_ADDITIONAL_ROUNDS:
TERMINATION_CONDITION:
```

Additional rounds must not repeat stable positions, manufacture consensus, delay a
decision for theoretical completeness, or broaden mission scope. Every extra round needs
a bounded decision-relevant purpose and must preserve original and minority positions.

This direction will be integrated into shared Council policy after the current mission
closes so the running Round 1 instruction baseline remains unchanged.

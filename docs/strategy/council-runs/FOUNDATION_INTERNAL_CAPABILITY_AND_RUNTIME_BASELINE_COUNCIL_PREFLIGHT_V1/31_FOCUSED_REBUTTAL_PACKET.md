# Focused Rebuttal Packet

```text
FOCUSED_REBUTTAL_REQUIRED: NO
REASON: Round 2 resolved every material inter-role disagreement into a common proposition and resolution condition. Additional Challenger argument cannot resolve the remaining Founder authority choices or factual repository/runtime/provider unknowns.
ROUND_3_EXECUTED: NO
ROUNDS_4_OR_5_EXECUTED: NO
ARTIFICIAL_CONSENSUS_SOUGHT: NO
MINORITY_POSITIONS_PRESERVED: YES
```

The deliberation terminates after two substantive rounds in accordance with the Council
protocol's focused-rebuttal condition. The unused round does not become automatic scope.

## Decision question

Is the proposed Foundation-wide baseline the smallest sufficient, safe, and
evidence-grounded investigation for determining how much of Foundation's ultimate
mission is actually implemented, and for producing a reviewed current-state map plus
a bounded implementation proposal without beginning implementation?

Return one verdict:

- `PROCEED_WITH_CORRECTIONS`
- `HOLD`
- `NOT_NEEDED`

## Founder direction to preserve

- Foundation is intended to be the shared canonical AI, knowledge, evidence,
  judgment, and safety core for SIASIU, Cosmile, and future services.
- Product knowledge should support atomic decomposition and future governed matching
  between people and products.
- Future learning from new evidence is a product direction, not authority for automatic
  canonical promotion or Memory V3 activation in this mission.
- Memory V3 remains paused.
- Cosmile Golden Commerce remains unchanged.
- Foundation must remain outside the synchronous path of ordinary catalog, cart,
  checkout, payment, order, fulfillment, and refund operations.

## Proposed later baseline boundary

After separate Leo approval, the later Advisor-led mission may inspect current
Foundation sources and historical foundation-control implementation, canonical Vault
and knowledge sources, provenance, versions, indexes, retrieval, reasoning, judgment,
safety behavior, models, provider/routing boundaries, memories, APIs, downstream
adapters, atomic product representation, matching readiness, and governed-learning
readiness.

It must report separate states for:

```text
SOURCE
BUILD
TEST
RUNTIME
INTEGRATION
AUTHORITY
TARGET_FIT
```

It may use isolated non-production build, test, API, retrieval, index, and synthetic
question execution. It may not change product code or canonical Vault data; access
shared/production data or PII; activate Memory V3 or live flags; or start an
implementation mission.

Historical foundation-control code is evidence of physical implementation only. It
does not acquire current Worker or product authority automatically.

## Required knowledge-use trace

The later baseline must test more than file existence and record counts. It must trace:

```text
canonical source
-> provenance and version
-> normalization or atomic representation
-> index inclusion
-> retrieval
-> reasoning, judgment, or safety use
-> response-level evidence
-> downstream consumption or explicit non-consumption
```

Missing evidence ends as `UNVERIFIED`; it must not be converted to a fact by Council
reasoning.

## Council-only investigation boundary

In this preflight, Challengers may read only Council root/shared rules, their own role
and runtime adapter, and the exact frozen mission files assigned to them. They must not:

- inspect Foundation, foundation-control, SIASIU, or Cosmile repositories;
- browse authoritative sources or vendor documentation;
- execute builds, tests, APIs, indexes, retrieval, synthetic questions, or runtimes;
- contact Advisor, Workers, Control, Designer, or Reviewer;
- modify the reviewed subject, role infrastructure, or any product repository;
- accept product, privacy, legal, security, architecture, or operational risk;
- claim independent design, implementation, security, or legal review.

## Unknown-handling rule

Use only the approved disposition taxonomy in the frozen register. Factual unknowns
cannot be resolved by Council analysis. Council analysis may resolve only strategy,
scope, architecture, prioritization, and risk reasoning.

## Deliberation and stopping

Round 1 is blind. Do not inspect or infer another Challenger's output. Later cross-review
will occur only after all selected Round 1 reports are frozen. Default deliberation is
three rounds; up to five are permitted only for exact unresolved material issues. Do
not seek artificial consensus.

After the Council result, Strategy must return the corrected bilingual Advisor
instruction candidate and consolidated result to Leo and stop. No Advisor dispatch or
Foundation baseline starts without a new explicit Leo approval.

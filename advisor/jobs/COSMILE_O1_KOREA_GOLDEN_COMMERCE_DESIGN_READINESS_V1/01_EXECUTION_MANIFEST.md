## Safety and repository discipline

- Product and Control repositories are read-only; preserve every pre-existing untracked
  file and exact tracked state.
- No build, lint, test, smoke, runtime, endpoint, DB connection, schema application,
  migration execution, PSP/auth integration, secret/PII access, transaction, provider
  contact, KYC, merchant activation, or product-branch mutation.
- Read-only source and Git-object inspection only. No dependency installation.
- All durable mission output is written only to the output worktree on the approved
  foundation-docs branch.
- Only exact mission paths are staged. Draft PR only; no merge.
- Every actor reports facts, assumptions, unknowns, evidence pins, zero product writes,
  and a compact pointer to the Advisor, then stops.
- Session recovery after a transient interruption must preserve actor, model, role,
  CWD, and approved effort; otherwise return a blocker.

## Stop conditions

Return to Strategy immediately only for an authority/evidence integrity failure,
unverifiable canonical Foundation source, prohibited access requirement, material
Korea/KRW or O1 reversal, broad-rewrite-only path, material scope expansion, binding
Legal/risk decision required before meaningful planning, or current role/repository
ownership conflict.

Routine unknowns, vendor confirmation gaps, and later Founder decisions are recorded
in the final package and do not stop read-only design work.

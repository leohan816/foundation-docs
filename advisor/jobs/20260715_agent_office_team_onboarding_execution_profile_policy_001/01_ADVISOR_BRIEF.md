# Advisor Brief

MISSION_ID: `AGENT_OFFICE_TEAM_ONBOARDING_AND_EXECUTION_PROFILE_POLICY_001`

RISK_CLASS: `LEVEL_3_AUTHORITY_ADJACENT_CONTRACT_AND_STATE_MODEL`

## Mission completion contract

The final reviewed candidate must provide one coherent canonical system for:

- Team-wide onboarding from one Advisor entry instruction;
- registry-only actor discovery and optional-role handling;
- role-specific onboarding/reload handoffs;
- actual read and understanding validation;
- immutable `PROTOCOL_READY` evidence and deterministic `TEAM_READY` aggregation;
- canonical-commit/version staleness and targeted re-onboarding;
- reviewed registration before a new Actor becomes dispatchable;
- per-dispatch execution profile selection and explicit escalation;
- centralized role/onboarding authority under `docs/agent/`;
- a project entry-pointer contract without copying role manuals;
- targeted tests for all twelve Founder scenarios;
- operational instructions and exact reusable one-sentence commands.

## Invariants

1. Preserve every existing `roleInstanceId` and evidence join.
2. Do not infer capabilities or readiness from actor/session/project names,
   current process alone, terminal prose, or visual proximity.
3. Do not overload changing readiness into the static registry.
4. Do not use the existing global allowed-token arrays as proof that every
   Actor supports the same model, mode, effort, or skill.
5. Missing optional roles are skipped. Missing/unready required registered
   actors block `TEAM_READY`.
6. Reviewer judgment remains independent while assignment/results route through
   the responsible Advisor.
7. Actor self-profile changes are rejected; every profile change requires a new
   Advisor dispatch record.
8. Capability insufficiency and transient operational failure are distinct.
9. No live onboarding rollout, Slack, secret, or tmux-delivery activation.

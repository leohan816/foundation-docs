# Fable5 Level-3 Living Pixel-Office Design Review Brief

Status: `WAIT_FOR_WORKER_DESIGN_RESULT`

The same existing `reviewer-fable5` session must independently read the final
design commit, actual canonical files, current source, dependency state, and
Leo/GPT decision. It must verify product-intent fidelity, renderer feasibility,
originality, evidence truth, accessibility/static equivalence, security and M1
authority preservation, deterministic visual testing, performance/cleanup,
rollback, and the thirteen-scene visual matrix.

It must specifically challenge whether the proposed primary viewport is truly a
game world rather than cards arranged inside a canvas; whether sprites support
the required behaviors; whether Channy stays non-operational; whether all Teams
coexist spatially; whether camera controls remain presentational; and whether the
DOM accessibility mirror can diverge from the Pixi world.

It must also verify that the prototype is isolated and synthetic, that a clean
design PASS cannot be interpreted as full runtime authorization, and that the
20-to-30-second recording plan proves motion rather than relying only on still
screenshots.

Verdict: `PASS | PASS_WITH_RISK | NEEDS_PATCH | FAIL`.

No implementation may start before a clean `PASS`. `PASS_WITH_RISK` returns to
Leo/GPT. The Reviewer does not patch or grant final approval.

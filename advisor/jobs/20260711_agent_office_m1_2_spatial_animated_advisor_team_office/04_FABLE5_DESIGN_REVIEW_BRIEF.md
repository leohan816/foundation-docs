## Review Focus

Fable5 must verify the exact committed design and diff, not trust summaries.

1. Every AO12-U01 through AO12-U14 is preserved and traceable.
2. Channy, art style, and asset source are not invented as facts.
3. Single Advisor Team Principle cannot duplicate, transfer, or visually imply
   authority.
4. Worker and Reviewer independence remains explicit in both hierarchy and
   animation.
5. Dynamic Team Pods scale beyond the current fixed eight-station union without
   silently breaking M1 contracts.
6. Current M1 structured-event truth, accepted IDs, precedence, stale/offline
   suppression, exact delivery, and completion evidence remain authoritative.
7. Ambient, lounge, and idle motion cannot imply work or collaboration.
8. Project colors are supplemental and do not collide with severity/state colors.
9. Desktop, tablet, mobile, 320px, 200% text, keyboard, screen-reader, and
   reduced-motion designs are equivalent and testable.
10. Performance targets are measurable and no unsupported threshold is labeled
    proven.
11. M1 compatibility is additive and historical events are not reinterpreted.
12. All future implementation batches are explicitly unauthorized and include
    review/rollback/acceptance gates.
13. The design contains no hidden runtime, transport, network, DB, Hermes,
    authority, or browser-dispatch authorization.
14. Genuine Leo/GPT decisions are minimal, plain-language, and do not offload
    technical details that the Worker can resolve.
15. Canonical files are discoverable from `docs/FEATURE_INDEX.md` and do not
    duplicate foundation-docs governance as product truth.

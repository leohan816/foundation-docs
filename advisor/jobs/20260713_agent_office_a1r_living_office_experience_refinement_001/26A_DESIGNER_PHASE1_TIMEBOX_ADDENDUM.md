# Designer Phase 1 Timebox Correction

This addendum supersedes any slower or more exhaustive interpretation of the
Phase 1 brief.

## Purpose

Phase 1 is rapid visual-direction exploration, not final design production. It
answers only: **Which living-office visual direction should be developed
further?**

## Timebox

- Target completion: `30-45 minutes` from initial Designer dispatch.
- Hard maximum: `60 minutes`.
- Return the best available directions at the hard limit; do not continue
  polishing.

## Output

- Produce two or three clearly differentiated, high-fidelity product-scene
  directions sufficient for Founder selection.
- Generate directions in parallel where the available image-generation tool
  permits independent calls without agents or sub-agents.
- Allow at most one regeneration pass per direction.
- Imperfect but clearly differentiated product directions are acceptable.

## Do Not Spend Time On

- pixel-perfect refinement;
- final labels or operational data;
- exhaustive annotations;
- SVG, HTML, or presentation-board construction;
- complete desktop composition;
- motion production;
- contract or geometry review.

Use image-generation capability. Do not manually construct the scenes as SVG,
HTML, CSS, or presentation diagrams. Preserve every authority, implementation,
Worker, Reviewer, and later-phase prohibition from the main brief.
